## Problem

When an **ApplicationSet** lives outside the `argocd` namespace (e.g. `team-platform`) and the control plane is configured to manage that namespace via `application.namespaces`, the **ApplicationSet detail UI does not load**.

**Public repro URL (from reporter):**  
https://cd.apps.argoproj.io/applicationsets/team-platform/services

**Expected:** Detail view shows the AppSet and generated Applications (screenshot in issue).  
**Actual:** View fails / does not load for namespaced AppSets.

**Labels:** `bug`, `component:ui/appset`

---

## Why it matters

Enterprise GitOps often places AppSets in team namespaces. Broken UI blocks day-to-day ops (health, sync, drill-down) for exactly the multi-namespace pattern used at scale.
